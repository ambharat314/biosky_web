How It Works

From space to decision.
Four seamless steps.

1

Observe

Satellites capture Earth observation data — solar irradiance, cloud cover, atmospheric conditions, and surface temperatures across target regions.

2

Process

Raw data is ingested and processed through our cloud infrastructure, cleaning, normalizing, and structuring it for AI model consumption.

3

Analyze

Proprietary AI models — deep learning, graph neural networks, and reinforcement learning — generate forecasts, detect anomalies, and model scenarios.

4

Deliver

Actionable insights are delivered through our decision intelligence platform — real-time dashboards, APIs, and automated alerts for energy operators.
